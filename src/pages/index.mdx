# <FIRSTLEGO/> League Newfoundland and Labrador

<FIRSTLEGO/> League Newfoundland and Labrador (<FLLNL/>) is a division of Levitate Learning, a volunteer-driven,
not-for-profit organization based in St. John’s, Newfoundland and Labrador, Canada.

<FLLNL/> strives to promote interest in science, technology, engineering, and math (STEM) amongst youth in Newfoundland
and Labrador. For Inspiration and Recognition of Science and Technology (<FIRST/>) is a global non-profit organization
that was founded in 1989 and is headquartered in the United States. Through the delivery of their various robotics
programs and their adherence to their core values, <FIRST/> has been instrumental in exposing STEM to young people all over
the world, while developing students’ problem-solving, communication, teamwork, and leadership skills.

Newfoundland and Labrador has been participating in <FIRSTLEGO/> League since the early 2000s.
